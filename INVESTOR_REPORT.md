## 1. Executive Summary

**BrainwaveAI** is an advanced, adaptive AI learning platform designed to democratize elite-level tutoring. By leveraging a novel multi-agent architecture (LangGraph), Knowledge Graphs, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), BrainwaveAI provides hyper-personalized education that adapts to each student's learning style, pace, and knowledge gaps in real-time.

**The Problem:**
Traditional education is "one-size-fits-all." Private tutoring is effective but prohibitively expensive ($50-$200/hr). Existing AI tutors are often shallow wrappers around generic LLMs, lacking deep pedagogical context or long-term memory of a student's progress.

**The Solution:**
BrainwaveAI acts as an "Intelligent Orchestrator," employing specialized AI agents (Quiz Agent, Socratic Tutor, Visual Explainer) that collaborate to teach concepts deeply. It remembers every interaction, tracks knowledge mastery via a graph database, and evolves with the user.

**The Ask:**
We are seeking **$1.5 Million in Seed Funding** to finance 18 months of operations, enabling us to scale from MVP to 50,000 active users and achieve $1M ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue).
